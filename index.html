Conference Paper: Bridge Deck Repairs

This paper presents a novel adaptive learning strategy. The most accurate classification model from a heterogeneous classifier pool is selected at each learning epoch. Our goal is to achieve greater incrementla imrovement in accuracy, compared to using a single type of classification model throughout the process. The strategry is evaluated on benchmark data sets.
